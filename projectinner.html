V one

Branding

Client

V one is an agency that is specialized in the airplanes marketing field, and it is also the speed beyond which the takeoff should no longer be aborted in a scientific definition.

keywords

Branding/Stationary/Egypt/Graphic Design /Packaging/lines

client Brief

We need a visual identity that represents us as a very dynamic brand that is up to date with the most recent technologies and systems. With a really solid structure for the visual identity. Preferably to make something only in black and white to raise the brand recognition.

Concolusion

We came up with the agency logo from the scientific definition of Vone speed, that describes the speed from the taking off process when the speed is zero till its max and applied it to the logo. We also have created the theme of the project, pattern
and the art direction from the curved lines that reflect the speed and the dynamicality of the brand.
